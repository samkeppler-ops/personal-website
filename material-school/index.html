Our manifesto 💡

It feels like the world is rushing to put everything on a screen. Material School is about the material — the books, paper, supplies, spaces, and hands-on tools that help children learn — and how to give every student a material education in the AI era.

Let's figure out, together, the physical tools, materials, and supplies our kids most need to learn. By pairing rigorous research with real stories from the front lines, we're building a material school for the AI era.

Three pillars

🎒

🚚
